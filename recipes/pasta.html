Pasta

Description

My Pasta recipy. Unfortunatelly not very keto friendly, so I do not eat it any more.

Ingridients

Serves 1

  • Eggs, 1
  • Spaghetti Pasta, 50 gr
  • Cheddar, 40 gr (grated)
  • Onions, 2
  • Olive oil 10ml for pasta
  • Olive oil 10ml for sauce
  • Oregano or Thyme, to taste
  • Chilly Flakes, to taste

Steps

  1. Put water in a big pot and start heating it.
  2. Chop the Onions in small cubes
  3. Add the oil for the sause in a frying pan, and put to medium heat
  4. Add the onions, origano and chilly flakes to the frying pan.
  5. Stir until onions are soft, then turn the heat off.
  6. When the water starts boiling add the pasta and stir.
  7. When the pasta is done, drain, and rinse with cold water
  8. Add the oil for the pasta in the big pot and place under medium heat
  9. Once the oil is hot add the pasta and stir
  10. Add the onions, and stir
  11. Add the egg and stir quickly, so that the egg does not fry.
  12. Serve with cheese
