Fried eggs on a bed of yogurt

Description

A tasty way to start the day. The yogurt allows for the sausage to be spicy without overwhelming the senses.

If you do not use bread, the recipy is quite keto friendly. For best results use greek yogurt. I find that the tangines complements the fatty chorizo and cheddar cheese in the most intersting way.

Ingridients

Serves 1

  • Eggs, 2
  • Olive oil, 5 ml
  • Cheddar, 40 gr
  • Chorizo, 30 gr
  • Greek yogurt, 70 ml
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Oregano or Thyme, to taste
  • Chilly Flakes, to taste

Steps

  1. Chop the chorizo to thin quarted slices
  2. cut the cheddar into thin slices
  3. Spread the yogurt on a plate using a spoon. The aim is to create a bed of 2-5mm on which to later place the eggs
  4. Place the chorizo in a frying pan, and heat to medium heat.
  5. Wait for the chorizo fat to liquify, and add the olive oil.
  6. Break the eggs and place in the frying pan.
  7. Place the cheddar on top of the eggs.
  8. Add the thyme or oregano
  9. Periodically gather fat and oli from the side and drip on top of the eggs using a spoon.
  10. When eggs fried to your taste, place on the bed of yogurt
  11. Salt and pepper to taste.
